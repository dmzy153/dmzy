REIMAGINED Black

My First Project is here! This project REIMAGINED Black comes from a very natural place for me as an Artist.
I was going through pinterest, my interest was piqued by old art images, scultures ,paintings and even stories.
I remember thinking to myself why is a certain race not represented and how would they have been represented.
I began to reimagine some of these characrters differently, just like that an inspiration for a project.
I want people to see this and think, "how would they have been represented?". I really enjoyed making these pieces and i wanted these characters to carry emotion and struggles.
Hope you enjoy it as much i do!!!
